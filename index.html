Who are we?

Inventec AI Center is tasked with missions to advance the company's smart manufacturing capabilities, as well as to pioneer future AI technologies and products in areas as smart health, smart computing hardware, etc. In smart manufacturing, we've solved the problems of the logistic management process for forecasting parts needed for manufacturing the products, built systems for automatically qualifying products for mass production, and developed novel methodologies for efficiently creating AI models for visual inspection. In pioneering future AI technologies and products, we are investigating smart health for the wellbeing of mankind, smart computing including AI chips, architectures, and infrastructures for the ever-more demanding needs of AI computing.
